四种组件

Component Description
Camera This determines what is rendered on the screen
Lights These have an effect on how materials are shown and are used when creating shadow effects.
Objects These are the main objects that are rendered from the perspective of the camera, they include cubes, spheres, and point clouds.
Renderer Uses the camera and the information from the scene to draw the output on the screen.

Tips :

THREE.Scene serves as the container for the lights and the objects. But itself doesn’t have that many options and functions.
